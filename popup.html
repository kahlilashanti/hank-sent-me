What is the Hank Score?

The Hank Score estimates how strong a mutual connection really is—based on visibility, order, and interaction signals between profiles. We don’t access private LinkedIn data, but the score helps flag surface-level connections vs. ones with real depth.

A Hank Score above 90% usually means the person is well-positioned to broker an introduction. It's not flawless—but it’s a smarter signal than just a shared connection.
